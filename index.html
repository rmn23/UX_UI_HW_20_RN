Hi my name is Ruth!

I am a graduate UX/UI designer. I specialize in UX Research and Prototyping and enjoy the process of pairing together user and company needs in order to create an impactful product.

Memories Journalling App

I designed a journalling app for users who wanted a journalling app with a wellbeing aspect alongside two other UX Designers. This app achieved the goal of providing a space for users to journal consistently through notifications and a significant focus on wellbeing through daily affirmations and a reflective question of the day.

Beauty Hub App

I designed an app that is a platform for users to have access to information of all local beauty service providers including a range of customer reviews (voice note, video and written). With this app users can filter according to their unique preferences including hair type and hair texture. The app also provides evidence of certifications that beauty providers have acquired.
